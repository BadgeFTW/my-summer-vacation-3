My Summer Vacation

I worked all summer but on my days off i had a little fun. Me and my girlfriend went to ashville and to atlanta.

Ashville

Ashville is a great city with good cultural heritage and amazing views. We spent the day in ashville just relaxing walking around, before sunset we drove onto the blue ridge highway. It is an amazing road, really makes me wish i brought my car up there.

Atlanta

About a week after going to ashville we went to atlanta. It was pretty good as we went to the zoo and the aquarium then to downtown Atlanta.
